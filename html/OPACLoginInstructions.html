Some library users do not have CCA accounts:

  • Alumni whose CCA accounts have expired
  • Pre-college students
  • Visiting researchers sponsored by a CCA affiliate

If you fall in one of these categories, use the form below. Your username is surname.givenname (e.g. "jones.olivia" for someone named "Olivia Jones") and your password was initially set to your library barcode (something like PCAC9876543210X) but can be changed. Contact us if you experience any problems.
